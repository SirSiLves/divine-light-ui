How to play

This section will explain more about Divine-Light with his rules and how to play the game.

Divine-Light is a chess-like strategy game but with rays of light. Each player alternately draws one of their pawns. The figures may only be moved 1 space at a time. A move also counts if the figure is rotated by 90 °, for example. At the end of each turn, your own light beam must be activated. If the light beam hits a pawn on a non-mirrored side, it is removed from the game. This also applies if your own beam of light hits your own figure. If the light beam hits the king, the game is won or lost for the other player.

At the beginning each player has 13 pawns in the colors gold or silver. There are different types of figures. Some can either redirect or block the light.

A move consists of either moving a pawn in any direction (even diagonally) onto an immediately adjacent free space or rotating it.

The fields vertically above the opposing sun as well as certain other fields may not be entered, they are marked in color accordingly.

After moving, the light of God is activated. Since the figures are aligned at right angles on the playing field, the light always moves horizontally and vertically within the playing area.

Distracted by mirrors, he either hits the edge of the playing field or a non-mirrored side of a figure. In the latter case, this figure is removed from the game, even if it is your own.

If it hits the king, the game is over.

Reflector

The two reflector figures have a diagonal mirror on both sides. They reflect rays from all sides, deflect it by 90 degrees and cannot be hit.

However, they can also be useful to the enemy. The reflectors are the only ones allowed to swap places with certain directly adjacent figures of both colors.

Angler

The Winkler is only mirrored on one side of the diagonal and can therefore be hit from two of the four sides.

Wall

The stone wall has no mirrored sides. However, it has a filter and can therefore block the light on one side.

Sun

The light of God comes from the sun. This figure can only turn 90 ° in the direction of the board.

King

The king is the most important character in the game. It has no mirrored sides. If the king is hit, the player has lost.
