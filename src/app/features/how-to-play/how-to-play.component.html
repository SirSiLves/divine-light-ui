Reflector

The two reflector figures have a diagonal mirror on both sides. They reflect rays from all sides, deflect it by 90 degrees and cannot be hit.

However, they can also be useful to the enemy. The reflectors are the only ones allowed to swap places with certain directly adjacent figures of both colors.

Angler

The Winkler is only mirrored on one side of the diagonal and can therefore be hit from two of the four sides.

Wall

The stone wall has no mirrored sides.

Sun

The light of God comes from the sun. This figure can only turn 90 Â° in the direction of the board.

King

The king is the most important character in the game. It has no mirrored sides. If the king is hit, the player has lost.
